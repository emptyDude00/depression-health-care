About Us

Caring, confidential and professional

Depression Health Care is a South Africa‑based practice focused on depression, anxiety and burnout. We believe help should feel human, practical and hopeful.

Our Approach

We combine compassion with proven methods like CBT, ACT and solution‑focused therapy.

Values

Safety, dignity, respect and evidence‑based care guide every session.

Who We Help

Teens, students, adults and professionals facing persistent low mood, isolation, or loss of motivation.
